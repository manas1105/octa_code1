Facilities and Trainings


Basic football training focuses on developing fundamental skills, fitness, and understanding of the game. A typical session begins with a warm-up, including light jogging and dynamic stretches to prepare the body.

Shooting drills focus on close-range and long-range shots, improving technique, accuracy, and power. Basic defending is also covered, including 1v1 situations and defensive positioning.

Dribbling drills follow, with exercises like cone dribbling and change of direction to enhance ball control. Passing drills are next, emphasizing accurate passing and receiving, either with a partner or against a wall.
